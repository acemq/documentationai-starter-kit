## Getting Started

To create your AceMQ account:

1. Go to `https://portal.acemq.com/register`
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your email for the verification link
4. Click the link to activate your account

## Next Steps

- Log in at `https://portal.acemq.com`
- Add your company details under **Account Settings**
- Contact support for RabbitMQ licensing or consulting
