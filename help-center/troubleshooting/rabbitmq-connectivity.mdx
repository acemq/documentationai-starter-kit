---
title: RabbitMQ Connectivity Issues
description: Resolve connection problems to your RabbitMQ cluster.
---

## Symptoms

- Can't connect to RabbitMQ instance
- Timeout or connection refused errors

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check RabbitMQ status">
    ````bash
    sudo systemctl status rabbitmq-server
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify firewall rules">
    Ensure ports 5672 and 15672 are open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test credentials">
    Use `rabbitmqctl authenticate_user` to verify login.
  </Step>
</Steps>
