## Overview

Basic messaging in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start RabbitMQ">
    ````bash
    sudo systemctl start rabbitmq-server
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a message">
    ````bash
    rabbitmqadmin publish exchange=amq.default routing_key=test payload="Hello AceMQ!"
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the message">
    ````bash
    rabbitmqadmin get queue=test
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
